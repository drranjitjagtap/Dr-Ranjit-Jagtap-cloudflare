About Dr. Ranjit Jagtap

Dr. Ranjit Jagtap is a distinguished Cardiothoracic Surgeon in Pune with extensive experience in advanced heart and chest surgeries. Over the years, he has built a reputation for clinical excellence in complex cardiac procedures, including coronary artery bypass surgery and other critical heart interventions. His medical approach combines precision, innovation, and patient-centered care, ensuring improved outcomes for individuals facing serious cardiovascular conditions.

He is also the Founder of the Ram Mangal Heart Foundation, an initiative dedicated to providing affordable and accessible cardiac care to underserved communities. Through preventive screening programs, community outreach initiatives, and awareness campaigns, Dr. Ranjit Jagtap actively promotes early diagnosis and lifestyle-based prevention strategies. His commitment extends beyond the operating room, focusing on strengthening public awareness about heart health and encouraging proactive cardiovascular care across all age groups.
